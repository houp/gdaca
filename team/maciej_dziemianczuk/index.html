Non-uniform number-conserving elementary cellular automata on the infinite grid: a tale of the unexpected

Barbara Wolnik | Maciej Dziemiańczuk | Bernard Baets
Information Sciences, 2023 Journal
DOI BibTeX
@article{Wolnik2023b,
    author = "Wolnik, Barbara and Dziemiańczuk, Maciej and Baets, Bernard De",
    title = "Non-uniform number-conserving elementary cellular automata on the infinite grid: a tale of the unexpected",
    journal = "Information Sciences",
    volume = "649",
    pages = "119680",
    year = "2023",
    doi = "10.1016/j.ins.2023.119680",
    abstract = "Contrary to the finite case, non-uniform number-conserving cellular automata exhibit novel behavior on an infinite lattice. This study shows that when considered on an infinite grid, certain combinations of local rules can achieve global tasks that were impossible on finite grids. The authors identify a family of unexpected number-conserving rules unique to the infinite setting, providing an answer to an open question and revealing a sharp distinction between finite and infinite CA dynamics."
